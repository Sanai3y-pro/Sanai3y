#Sanai3y Project

Sanai3y is a professional web-based platform designed to bridge the gap between customers and skilled local craftsmen, including plumbers, electricians, carpenters, painters, and other trades. The platform provides a secure, organized, and user-friendly environment for service requests, communication, contracts, and payments, ensuring trust and transparency for all parties involved.

#Overview

The platform addresses the challenges of finding reliable local craftsmen by offering a centralized system where customers can search, request, and review services efficiently. Craftsmen can manage their offerings, verify their identity, and receive payments securely. Real-time communication, automated contracts, and notifications enhance the overall experience and ensure accountability.

#Core Technologies

Backend: ASP.NET Core
Database: SQL Server with Entity Framework Core
Frontend: Razor Pages / MVC Views, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

#Key Features

User Registration & Verification: Secure onboarding for customers and craftsmen.
Service Management: Publish, edit, and manage service offerings.
Search & Filters: Locate services by category, location, rating, or price.
Service Requests & Offers: Customers submit requests; craftsmen provide offers.
Contracts & Payments: Automated contract generation and secure payment processing.
Reviews & Ratings: Mandatory feedback after service completion.

#Policies & Guidelines

Craftsmen must publish at least one service to activate their account.
Identity verification is mandatory.
External contact sharing is prohibited.
All agreements and payments are to be conducted through the platform.
Reviews after service completion are mandatory.
Any attempt to bypass platform rules may result in account suspension or banning.

#Vision & Future Enhancements

Sanai3y aims to create a trusted, efficient, and professional ecosystem for local craftsmanship services. Future improvements may include:
AI-driven recommendations to match customers with suitable craftsmen.
Loyalty and reward systems for active users.
Integration of additional payment gateways and service categories.

#Conclusion

Sanai3y combines secure technology, organized processes, and a professional interface to revolutionize the local service marketplace. It ensures satisfaction, reliability, and trust for both customers and craftsmen, setting a new standard for digital craftsmanship platforms.
